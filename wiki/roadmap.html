Product Roadmap

Our vision for the future of Agent CLI. We're building the most powerful terminal-native AI workforce.

Q1 2026 (Completed)

V1 Launch

Initial release of the core orchestrator, TUI, and support for primary AI providers (Ollama, OpenAI, Gemini).

Q2 2026 (In Progress)

Advanced Autonomy & MCP

Implementation of "Mission Mode" for long-running tasks and deep integration with the Model Context Protocol (MCP) ecosystem.

Q3 2026

Multi-Agent Orchestration

Enable primary agents to spawn specialized sub-agents for specific tasks (e.g., a "Test Agent" or a "Refactor Agent") working in parallel.

Q4 2026

V2 Prototype

Moving beyond text: Vision capabilities for UI debugging and deeper IDE-level integrations.
